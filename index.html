Moonrisers

You and three other astroscientists are exploring a new planet when you awaken an ancient horror beneath the planet's surface. With your shuttle destroyed, you have no choice but to build upward to the moon while evading the creature's clutches and its mysterious mind-controlling abilities.

Moonrisers is couch co-op 2D platformer where players need to build their way to the moon! Each player is equipped with a gamepad controller, a tool they may lose if they die.

One unlucky player must use a tablet device. This player has only one goal. Kill the other Moonrisers. If they succeed, they take their place as a builder, and the fallen must now use the tablet.

Be the first to reach the moon. If you do, you have an option to leave, or try bring your friends with you. Which will you decide?
